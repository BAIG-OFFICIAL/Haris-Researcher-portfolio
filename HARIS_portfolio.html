01 · About

The Researcher
Behind the Work

Muhammad Haris Baig is a biomedical researcher currently working as a Research Assistant at Kwon's Stem Cell Lab - one of Asia's top-ranked research institutions - at Soonchunhyang University (SIMS), South Korea.

My research focuses on RNA-binding proteins in hematopoietic stem cell maintenance, leukemogenesis, cancer molecular biology, nanotechnology, and diagnostic sciences. I have authored multiple papers in international journals, reviewed multiple articles for various journals, and educate the next generation of biomedical professionals through my YouTube channel Medical Lab Tactics.
